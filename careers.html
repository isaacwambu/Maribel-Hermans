Internships

Maribel-Hermans provide opportunity for those undertaking professional course in finance, accounting, audit, tax and environmental studies. This is a three to six months program to allow the students chance to learn and improve their knowledge in their areas of studies through mentorship and coaching. Feel most welcome to explore, learn and share experience.

Experienced

We provide opportunities to those professionals with working experience and would like to experience new challenges in their areas of expertise and enhance their professional growth and development.

Trainee Program

We provide opportunity for those undertaking CPA course through mentorship, coaching and on job training. We are open to those who would like to have practical experience before becoming accountants as per the Accountant Act no. 15 of 2008. We partner with ICPAK in this program to enhance experience and knowledge among trainee accountants.
