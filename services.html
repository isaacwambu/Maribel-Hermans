Services

The following are the services offered by Maribel-Hermans Associates;-

1. Audit & Assurance

We provide quality audit and assurance services to our clients through expertise and experience of our audit teams. We maintain close communication with our clients to drive value from our services while maintaining regular and close interaction with management and audit committee to ensure that key objectives of the business are achieved. We apply modern technology, knowledge-based applications and in depth understanding of the audit process to deliver quality audits. We apply risk-based methodology in our financial audits and leverage on our professional and industry experience to deliver in our assignments.

2. Tax Planning & Preparation

We provide our clients with tax planning, preparation and compliance services to individuals, partnerships, pension schemes, religious organisations, Saccos and corporate businesses. We provide monthly VAT returns and annual filings to our clients and handle any matter related to tax. Visit our tax page, corporate and individual tax planning for more information.

4. Environmental Consulting

  • Environmental Impact Assessment

    • Individuals and businesses implement projects which require compliance with environmental laws. Maribel-Hermans provide Environmental Impact Assessment environmental solutions to our clients involved in project development both at individual and business levels in compliance with Environmental Management and Coordination Act (EMCA), 1999. Out team expertise are at the disposal of clients with requisite tools to undertake environmental impact assessment while developing sustainable environmental management plans to mitigate against environmental risks the projects might be exposed to.

  • Strategic Environmental and Social Assessment

    • Organization periodic development plans including strategic and spatial plans. The strategic goals and objectives must be incorporated with sustainable development goals where environmental considerations are included. Maribel-Hermans are privileged to be endowed with knowledge, skills and expertise on strategic environmental and social assessment which is critical in the planning process. We provide workable and lasting solutions that embed strategic with environmental goals.

  • Environmental Audits and Monitoring

    • Environmental scanning and assessment is critical for organisations and individual investors. Our dedicated team leverage on the experience, expertise, skills and knowledge of various industries to help organisations to assess their environmental conditions. Maribel-Hermans provide high quality environmental risk-based assessments, audits while developing environmental management plans in compliance with EMCA Act, 1999 and advising businesses on the need to maintain sustainable environmental practices. In addition, we partner with clients in the implementation of environmental management systems conducive to working conditions.

  • Health & Safety Services

    • Health and safety is critical in the operation and management of organizations. Good, healthy and safe working environment is good for businesses, employees and service providers and it can boost productivity and limit damages and claims that may arise from incidents. This leads to low operating costs while maintaining and attracting new business opportunities. Organisations have moral obligations to provide healthy and safe working environment, and in compliance with the Occupational Safety and Health Act (OSHA), 2007. Maribel-Hermans offer solutions through the following services.

    • Health and Safety audits
    • Developing of safety policies
    • Safety inspection of development sites

    We further review condition and work environment to minimize workplace incidents that may increase occupational risk resulting to increased cost to the businesses.
